Chicken Noodle Casserole

Ingredients

  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (optional topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions, drain and set aside
  3. Mix cream of mushroom soup and milk in a large bowl
  4. Add chicken, noodles, vegetables, and cheese to soup mixture
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Pour into greased 9x13 inch baking dish
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until heated through
  8. Top with breadcrumbs and bake 5 more minutes if desired

Servings

Serves 6-8 people

Cooking Time

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 35 minutes | Total time: 50 minutes

Notes

Feel free to customize with your favorite vegetables or cheese varieties!
