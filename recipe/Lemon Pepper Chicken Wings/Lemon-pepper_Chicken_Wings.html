Lemon-Pepper Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken wings
  • 2 tbsp lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil e.g olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except olive oil.
  3. Drizzle cooking oil over the mixture and toss to coat.
  4. Place wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.

Servings

Serves 4 people


Cooking Time

Prep time: 10 minutes | Cook time: 30 minutes | Total time: 40 minutes


Notes

For extra crispiness, broil the wings for the last 2-3 minutes after baking. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve with your favorite dipping sauce!
