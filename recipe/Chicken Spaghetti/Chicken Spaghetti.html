Chicken Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Long-Strand Pasta: Use Spaghetti, Linguine, Fettuccine or any other long pasta.
  • 2 cups Shredded Chicken. For 5-star flavor, use Rotisserie Chicken (dark and white meat mix) or pan-seared chicken thighs.
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika: Adds a subtle wood-fired depth.
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder & 1 tsp Onion Powder: Essential for base flavor.
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning: A blend of oregano, basil, and thyme.
  • ¼ tsp Cayenne or Red Pepper Flakes.
  • 1 cloves Fresh Garlic (minced) and ¼ cup Diced Onion.
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) Condensed Cream of Chicken (with Herbs if available).
  • 1 can (10 oz) Diced Tomatoes with Green Chiles (undrained for moisture).
  • Use Sharp Cheddar or a Colby Jack blend instead of processed cheese cubes for a more sophisticated texture.
  • Salt, Cracked Black Pepper, and fresh Parsley for garnish.

Instructions

  1. Sauté the Base: In a large pan, melt 1 tbsp of butter. Sauté the fresh onion and garlic until fragrant (about 2 minutes).
  2. Season with Sauce: Stir in the cream of chicken, undrained tomatoes, and your entire seasoning blend. Whisk until smooth and bubbling.
  3. Cheesing up: Fold in the shredded cheese (about 1.5 to 2 cups) until the sauce is velvety.
  4. The Mixing: Add the cooked chicken and your pasta. Toss vigorously over low heat.
  5. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of Chicken Broth or reserved Pasta Water to make it "silkier".
  6. Garnish: Finish with a heavy crack of black pepper and fresh parsley

Servings

Serves 4-6 people

Cooking Time

Prep time: 15 minutes | Cook time: 20 minutes | Total time: 35 minutes

Notes

This Chicken Spaghetti is creamy, cheesy, and slightly spicy dish that's served as my weeknight dinners or go to for casual gatherings. For an extra layer of flavor, consider adding sautéed mushrooms or bell peppers to the base. Leftovers also make for fantastic sandwiches or can be baked with an additional layer of cheese on top for a delicious casserole the next day
